Hello

My name is Joe, I'm a UK based physics educator, hobbyist web developer and athlete/official
(but people often tell me I should be a comedian)

Things I get up to

Education

Much of my time is spent teaching physics at A Level & GCSE in addition to science at KS3. I have close links with the Institute of Physics and make a real effort to interest people in what they are learning.

Web development

My web development hobby stems from my interest for sharing knowledge; I'm particularly interested in free and open source software. I'm an active member of the Drupal community and have been building Drupal websites since 2009.

Athletics

I've been doing athletics since the early 2000s. I'm a qualified technical official and do a lot of volunteer work for the sport while still finding time to do a bit of running myself.
(Claim to fame: I once held the UK record for speed bounce)
